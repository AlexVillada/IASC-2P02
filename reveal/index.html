First Wax Cylinders

  • Were made of ceresin, beeswax and stearic wax
  • Vibrations from sound into horn > would vibrate needle > needle engraves cylinder
  • Smaller cylinders // 2 minutes of audio
  • Bigger cylinders // 4 minutes of audio
  • Had 2 diaphragm/needle units, one for input, one for output(vinyl)

Advancements from Wax
